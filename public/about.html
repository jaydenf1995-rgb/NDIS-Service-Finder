About NDIS Service Finder

Connecting NDIS participants with trusted service providers across Australia

Our Story

NDIS Service Finder was born from a simple observation: finding the right NDIS services shouldn't be complicated. As someone deeply familiar with the NDIS landscape, I saw firsthand how challenging it can be for participants and their families to navigate the system and connect with quality providers.

What started as a personal project to help a few people in my local community has grown into a comprehensive directory serving NDIS participants across Australia. Our mission is to make the process of finding and connecting with service providers as simple and transparent as possible.

What We Offer

Comprehensive Directory

Access to a wide range of NDIS-approved service providers including support workers, coordinators, therapists, and specialist services.

Verified Information

All listed providers include essential details like NDIS registration status, service areas, and contact information.

User Reviews

Read genuine feedback from other participants to make informed decisions about service providers.

Easy Search & Filters

Find exactly what you need with our intuitive search and filtering system by location, service type, and more.

Free Access

Our platform is completely free for both participants and service providers because access to essential services shouldn't come with a price tag.

Community Focused
