Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

1. Introduction

NDIS Service Finder ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and handling your information in accordance with the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs) under the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use our website or engage with our services.

By using our website, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We may collect personal information when you:

  • Submit your service through Google Forms
  • Contact us directly
  • Submit reviews or feedback
  • Subscribe to email updates

2.2 Service Provider Information

Service provider submissions may include:

  • Business name and ABN (if provided)
  • Contact details (email, phone)
  • Service categories and descriptions
  • NDIS registration status (as provided by you)
  • Location, regions serviced, and availability

Important: We do not independently verify NDIS registration status. Providers are responsible for ensuring their information is accurate.

2.3 Automatically Collected Information

When you browse the site, we may collect:

  • IP address and approximate location
  • Device type, browser type, and operating system
  • Pages viewed, session duration, and referral sources
  • Interactions with ads (after AdSense is enabled)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and operate the directory platform
  • Display service provider information publicly
  • Improve website performance and user experience
  • Respond to enquiries and provide support
  • Display personalised or contextual ads
  • Ensure security, prevent misuse, and detect fraud
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations

We do not use your information for any purpose unrelated to the operation of this directory unless legally required or you have provided explicit consent.

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may disclose information in the following circumstances:

  • Public Listings: Provider submissions (excluding sensitive personal details) are displayed publicly as part of the directory.
  • Third-Party Services: Information may be shared with hosting, analytics, or advertising providers such as Google and Vercel.
  • Legal Compliance: If required by law, court order, or regulatory authorities.
  • Platform Protection: To prevent fraud, misuse, or security threats.

5. Data Storage and Security

We implement reasonable technical and administrative measures to safeguard your information. However, no internet transmission is fully secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data submissions through Google Forms are stored on Google's servers and are governed by Google’s Privacy Policy.

Website hosting is provided by Vercel, which may store data in overseas data centres such as the United States or the European Union.

6. Data Retention

We retain information:

  • For as long as required to operate the directory
  • As required for legal or regulatory purposes
  • Until a provider requests removal or amendment

You may request the deletion of your data at any time.

7. Your Rights

You have rights under the Australian Privacy Principles, including:

  • Access to personal information we hold about you
  • Correction of inaccurate, incomplete, or outdated information
  • Deletion of your information upon request
  • Withdrawal of consent (where applicable)
  • Opt-out of marketing or advertising communications

To exercise any right, email us at: privacy@ndisservicefinder.com
